How it works

  1. Uploading

    Upload your audio or video files to Lexoral. We process the audio to improve the clarity, then send it off to be transcribed by Google's state of the art AI

  2. Perfecting

    Even the best AI makes mistakes, but with Lexoral it's easy to correct them. Click around the transcript and hear the audio play in sync. No more swapping back and forth between the audio and text

  3. Exporting

    Your edits are automatically saved online, but you can download a copy. Pick a format that suits you, whether that's plain-text, pdf, or even a subtitles file for videos
