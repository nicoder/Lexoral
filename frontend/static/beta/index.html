About the Beta

Lexoral is already incredibly useful, but it's still early stages.

There are bugs we haven't caught and ideas we haven't thought of.

We need people like you to try Lexoral and really get a feel for it.

Then, we'll use your valuable feedback to make Lexoral better for everyone!

As long as you're in the beta program, you can transcribe as much as you want for free.

In exchange, just let us know what's annoying you about Lexoral!

Who can sign up?

We'll read and consider every application, but the ideal applicant would be:

  • A UK-based student or academic
  • Experienced with transcription
  • Ready to use Lexoral frequently
  • Excited to make Lexoral great

If that's not you, don't let it put you off!

Get in touch and let's chat about how we can work together.
